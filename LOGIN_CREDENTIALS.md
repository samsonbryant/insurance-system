## üìã Access Levels by Role

### Admin
- Full system access
- User management (create, edit, delete users)
- Company management and approvals
- Policy management
- Verification management
- Reports and analytics
- System settings
- Audit logs

### Officer
- Policy verification via QR code or manual search
- View verification history
- Access to verification dashboard
- Real-time verification notifications

### Insurer/Company
- Company dashboard
- Policy management (create, view, edit policies)
- Claims management
- Verification logs for their company
- Reports and analytics for their company
- Company profile management

### CBL (Central Bank of Liberia)
- Company registration and approval
- Policy approval and oversight
- Regulatory reports
- System-wide analytics
- Company status management

### Insured
- View own policies
- Submit claims
- View claim status
- Policy verification history
- Personal profile management

## üîí Security Notes

1. **Default Passwords:** All users should change their default passwords after first login for security purposes.

2. **Password Requirements:**
   - Minimum 6 characters
   - Recommended: Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters

3. **Login Methods:**
   - Users can log in using either **username** or **email address**
   - Both methods are supported

4. **Account Status:**
   - All accounts listed above are active by default
   - Inactive accounts cannot log in

5. **Password Reset:**
   - Contact system administrator for password reset
   - Password reset functionality available for active users
